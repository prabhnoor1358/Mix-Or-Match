About The Game

Welcome to the website,
where you can play a fun and challenging card flip mix or match game.
The game is simple: you have to find all the matching pairs of cards in 2 minutes or less.
Sounds easy, right? But there's a twist: the cards are shuffled and flipped over, so you can only see their backs.
You have to use your memory and logic skills to remember where each card is and flip them over to reveal their faces.
The game has only one level, but it's not as easy as it seems.
You have to deal with 16 cards, which means 8 pairs to match. The game will show you the number of flips you made, so you can try to beat your own record.
The game is designed to be fun and engaging for people of all ages.
It can help you improve your memory, concentration, and cognitive abilities.
I hope you enjoy playing the game.
